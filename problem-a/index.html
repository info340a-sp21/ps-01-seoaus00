John Wick

John Wick is part of the John Wick trillogy starring Keanu Reeves. John Wick is a highly trained assassin and throughout the course of the three movies spends his time seeking revenge on those who have wronged him. The most recent film was John Wick: Chapter 3 - Parabellum.

Reasons why I admire John Wick and maybe why you should too:

  • Took down an entire crime family because one member kicked his dog...aka he loves dogs
  • His nickname is "Baba Yaga" or more people might recognize it as "The Boogeyman"...aka he's a beast
  • He has a great beard, and looks incredible in a suit
